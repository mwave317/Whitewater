Our Mission

Dedicated to promoting healthy and active lifestyles and developing environmental stewardship, the USNWC is home to the world’s largest man-made whitewater river, offering premier outdoor activities including whitewater rafting and kayaking, flatwater kayaking, mountain biking, rock climbing, zip lines, a canopy tour, rope courses and various team building activities. The Day Pass is the most popular way to experience the USNWC, offering access to available activities on the day of a visit.

Come experience our Tours

Tours provide guided experiences, in smaller groups, on a scheduled basis. Since Tours are reservation-based, guests can enjoy the tour activities without having to wait in line. Each Tour is booked in advance and departs at a specified time.

Daily Activity Schedule

The trail system is constantly monitored and is opened or closed as conditions permit or require. Current status is posted at various locations with Red (closed) or Green (open) flags.

OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES

The USNWC’s 1,300 acres offers a wide variety of outdoor activities for all ages and skill levels. Guests can enjoy whitewater rafting and kayaking, flatwater kayaking, stand-up paddle boarding, rock climbing, zip lines, ropes courses, a canopy tour, and mountain biking on our 30+ mile trail system.

FACILITY & TRAIL MAP

The USNWC is situated on over 1,300 acres of woodlands, and contains over 40 miles of trail for hiking, mountain biking, and trail running.
